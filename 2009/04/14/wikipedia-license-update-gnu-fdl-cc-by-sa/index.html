Wikipedia License Update - GNU FDL + CC-BY-SA

CC-BY 2.0

The Wikimedia Foundation is currently holding a vote on the possibility of dual licensing the content of the various Wikipedias. To qualify to vote you must hold a Wikipedia account with at least 25 edits prior to March 15. If the vote passes, there will no longer be a licensing conflict between Wikipedia and CC licensed works.
